Different Types of Images used on the Internet

There are 4 types of different images that are primarily used on the internet. Those are as follows:

  • JPG Images
  • GIF Images
  • PNG Images
  • SVG Images

Each of these different type of image files serve different purposes. They tend to be very good at serving the purpose that they were created for.

  1. JPG Images

    2. JPG images are designed for handling large color palettes without exorbitantly increasing file size. This makes them great for photos that have lots of gradients.

  2. GIF Images

    3. GIFs are great when it comes to simple animations, however, due to their limited color palette, they don't have a high level of detail. This is the biggest downside to this image file type

  3. PNG Images

    4. PNG images are great for anything that's not a photo OR animation. They tend to be larger than JPG files, but also don't have a limit on their color palette,which makes them great for dealing with opacity.

  4. SVG Images

    5. SVG images aren't pixel based, instead they are vector based. Because they are vector based, they can be scaled to any size without losing picture quality.
